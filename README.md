Solitaire 3-2-1 "Father's Edition"

A unique Klondike Solitaire variant loved by my late father. The game offers a perfect balance of challenge and accomplishment, starting hard and gradually becoming easier as you progress.

🎴 How to Play

The basic rules are the same as standard Klondike Solitaire. However, the game progresses in 3 phases:

Draw 3 : Draw 3 cards at a time.
Draw 2 : Once the stock is empty, it recycles, and you draw 2 cards.
Draw 1 : Recycles again, drawing 1 card at a time.

⚠️ Game Over: If you empty the stock in the 'Draw 1' phase, the game is over!

📖 The Story Behind This Game

When I was a child, I thought this was the official rule of Solitaire. Recently, I found my late father's old playing card rulebook. He had crossed out the original "always draw 3" rule and hand-written "then 2, then 1" next to it.

I don't know if it was truly his original rule, but he must have been very confident in this way of playing to go out of his way to rewrite it. He played this game every single day until the cards wore out—and then he would buy a new deck—saying it was a great mental exercise to keep his mind sharp.

For me, this rule is a cherished memory of my late father. I hope you enjoy playing it too.

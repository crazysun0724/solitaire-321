Solitaire 3-2-1 "Father's Edition"

[Play the Game Here!](https://crazysun0724.github.io/solitaire-321/)

A unique Klondike Solitaire variant loved by my late father. The game offers a perfect balance of challenge and accomplishment, starting hard and gradually becoming easier as you progress.

亡き父が愛した特別ルールのソリティア。最初は難しく、進むにつれて徐々に解きほぐされていく、絶妙な難易度と達成感が味わえるクロンダイクです。

🎴 How to Play

The basic rules are the same as standard Klondike Solitaire. However, the game progresses in 3 phases:
基本ルールは通常のクロンダイク・ソリティアと同じですが、以下の3つのフェーズで進行します：

Draw 3 (3枚めくり): Draw 3 cards at a time.

Draw 2 (2枚めくり): Once the stock is empty, it recycles, and you draw 2 cards.

Draw 1 (1枚めくり): Recycles again, drawing 1 card at a time.

⚠️ Game Over: If you empty the stock in the 'Draw 1' phase, the game is over!
（「1枚めくり」のフェーズで山札が尽きるとゲームオーバーです！）

📖 The Story Behind This Game

I learned this special rule from my late father and grew up believing it was the official way to play. Even after discovering the standard rules, they never quite felt right—this version will always be my "official" Solitaire.

亡き父から教わったこのルールを、私は子供の頃ずっと「公式ルール」だと思って育ちました。普通のルールを知った今でも、私の中での「公式」はこの遊び方です。

<summary><b>✨ Midnight Lights and an Unopened Deck / 真夜中の明かりと、未開封のトランプ</b></summary>





This "3-2-1" rule was our "house rule," played daily by my late father. When I was a child, I would sometimes wake up in the middle of the night and see a faint light leaking from his door. Peeking in, I would see him quietly playing Solitaire all by himself. He played until the cards were worn out, immediately replacing them with a new deck. Because of this, we always had a spare, unopened deck of cards at home...







この「3-2-1」というルールは、私の亡き父が毎日遊んでいた、わが家の“ハウスルール”です。子供の頃、夜中にふと目を覚ましてトイレに立つと、父の部屋のドアの隙間からうっすらと明かりが漏れていて、そっと覗くと父が一人、静かにソリティアに興じている。父はトランプがボロボロになるまで遊び、傷むとすぐに新しいデッキに替えるので、家にはいつも予備の、未開封のトランプがありました…。
